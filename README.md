#  Arclight

## AI Dependency Intelligence & Resilience Platform

**Mapping AI ecosystems, measuring dependency risks, and simulating disruptions before they happen.**

> "Know Your Dependencies Before They Become Your Failures."

---

##  Overview

Organizations increasingly rely on AI models, cloud providers, databases, and external APIs to power critical business workflows.

However, most organizations cannot answer:

- What AI services are we dependent on?
- Which provider is a single point of failure?
- What happens if a critical AI service becomes unavailable?
- How resilient is our AI ecosystem?

**Arclight** provides visibility into AI ecosystems by mapping dependencies, measuring risk exposure, simulating disruptions, and generating resilience recommendations.

---

##  Problem Statement

AI adoption is growing rapidly across enterprises, governments, financial institutions, and critical infrastructure.

While organizations have:

✅ Cybersecurity dashboards

✅ Cloud monitoring tools

They lack:

❌ AI Dependency Intelligence

❌ AI Resilience Visibility

❌ AI Failure Impact Assessment

This creates significant operational, regulatory, and business continuity risks.
